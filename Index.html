New College Stamford

New College Stamford teaches a variety of high quality full time, part time, university level and Apprenticeship courses. The College has heavily invested in its facilities in recent years and as a result students study amongst state-of-the-art facilities that are recommended by employers and industry professionals as those required in the working world. Our reputation for supporting students to achieve their university goals and dream jobs is founded on our success and excellent links with local employers. Choosing to study at New College Stamford is an exciting time where you'll find opportunities to develop knowledge and gain valuable experiences that will shape the rest of your life. Study what you love and don't be afraid to utilise our services to support your next steps. We look forward to meeting you!
